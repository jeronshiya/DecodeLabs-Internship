## Overview
This project focuses on understanding the needs, behaviors, motivations, and pain points of users before starting the design process. The goal is to develop a user-centered approach by conducting UX research and creating an empathy map.

## Scenario
A new fitness startup wants to launch a mobile application for busy office professionals who struggle to find time for exercise. Before designing the application, it is important to understand the users and their challenges.

## Objective
- Identify the target audience
- Create a detailed user persona
- Analyze user goals and frustrations
- Build an empathy map
- Discover key pain points and opportunities

## Target Audience
- Age Group: 25–45 Years
- Office Professionals
- IT Employees
- Corporate Workers
- Remote and Hybrid Employees

## User Persona
Name: Arjun Kumar
Age: 29
Occupation: Software Engineer

### Goals
- Maintain a healthy lifestyle
- Exercise regularly
- Improve fitness and energy levels

### Frustrations
- Lack of time for workouts
- Long working hours
- Difficulty maintaining consistency

## Pain Points
- Busy work schedule
- Lack of motivation
- Inconsistent fitness routine
- Sedentary lifestyle

## Key Insights
- Users need short and effective workouts
- Personalized fitness plans improve engagement
- Reminders help maintain consistency
- Flexible schedules are important for working professionals

## Tools Used
- Canva
- Figma

## Outcome
This project helped in understanding user needs, challenges, and expectations before starting the design process. The findings will support the creation of a user-friendly and effective fitness application.
